

@ Inspect Reboot Time-
* You can check when the system reboot happened with "who" and "last" commands.

Example 1- $ who -b
system boot 2021-02-13 20:51

Example 2- $ last -x | head | tac
esds pts/0 192.168.1.16 Sat Feb 13 19:53 - 19:55 (00:02)
reboot system boot 3.10.0-1160.11.1 Sat Feb 13 19:55 - 20:54 (00:58)
runlevel (to lvl 3) 3.10.0-1160.11.1 Sat Feb 13 19:55 - 20:04 (00:08)

# Check System Messages-
You can further correlate the reboot you want to diagnose with system messages.

# For CentOS/RHEL systems, /var/log/messages 
or /var/log/secure

# Ubuntu/Debian systems, /var/log/syslog. 
or
/var/log/auth.log
You can simply use the tail command or your favorite text editor to filter out or find specific data.
