# WHAT IS A DDOS ATTACK?
A Denial of Service (DoS) attack is a malicious attempt to affect the availability of a targeted system, such as a website or application.
In general, DDoS attacks can be happened Open Systems Interconnection (OSI) Network (layer 3), Transport (Layer 4), Presentation (Layer 6) and Application (Layer 7) Layers.
